Cookies are one of the most beloved treats around the world, known for their sweet aroma and comforting texture. Whether chewy, crispy, or soft, cookies come in countless varieties to satisfy every craving. From classic chocolate chip to oatmeal raisin, peanut butter, and sugar cookies, they are a versatile dessert that can be customized with endless ingredients like nuts, dried fruits, and spices. Often enjoyed with a glass of milk, a warm cookie straight from the oven brings a sense of nostalgia and warmth, making it a go-to comfort food for many. Their simplicity and ability to bring joy make cookies a timeless snack that never goes out of style.

  • Unsalted Butter
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Packed Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  1. Preheat and Prep: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cream Butter and Sugars: In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add Eggs and Vanilla: Beat in the eggs, one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract.
  4. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add this to the wet ingredients, mixing until combined.
  5. Scoop and Bake: Stir in any mix-ins (like chocolate chips or nuts). Scoop tablespoon-sized dough portions onto the baking sheet, spacing them apart. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden around the edges. Let cool before serving.
